### 📈 Trading Philosophy

> **Edge comes from information asymmetry and speed.**
>
> - Weather markets: Models update before prices adjust → **information gap**
> - BTC 15-min markets: Binance data is faster than Polymarket traders → **speed gap**
> - Always use **conservative sizing** (Quarter-Kelly) and strict risk management
> - Paper trade first, validate with data, then go live
